# Adticks Landing V2 Research

## Competitive Patterns Studied

- Ahrefs: strong category language around search, AI, and web discovery; product depth is communicated through tools and data, not generic SEO advice.
- Semrush: broad suite framing with explicit AI visibility workflows; clear path from monitoring to optimization.
- Botify: enterprise confidence around organic search automation, crawl intelligence, and large-site operations.
- Screaming Frog: technical trust through crawler specificity, JavaScript rendering, extraction, and dense diagnostics.
- Sitebulb: audit education, issue prioritization, crawl maps, and response-versus-render understanding.
- Ubersuggest: low-friction domain-first entry and simple wording for fast activation.
- Moz Pro: familiar all-in-one SEO platform framing around rankings, site crawl, and optimization workflows.

## Product Positioning Decision

Adticks should not be positioned as another SEO audit tool.

Primary category:

> Search and AI visibility measurement platform

Core promise:

> Instrument organic visibility across search engines, AI answers, technical crawl/render systems, content portfolios, internal authority, and competitive movement.

This lets Adticks grow into crawler intelligence, AI visibility tracking, content scoring, SERP intelligence, and agentic workflows without trapping the brand inside "site audit".

## UI/UX Decisions

- Domain-first hero because the market has trained users to start by entering a URL.
- Login stays available, but the primary story starts with measurement value, not account creation.
- Dark console-like visual language because the logo and product positioning fit infrastructure-grade measurement better than a light SaaS brochure.
- No customer logos, fabricated numbers, fake testimonials, or copied competitor screenshots.
- Product credibility comes from visible signals: crawl loss, AI answer share, rendered content mismatch, authority leak, content decay, and impact-ranked findings.
- Engineering stack names stay out of the conversion path; enterprise architecture can be documented separately.

## Content Architecture

1. Hero: Adticks as the measurement platform for search and AI visibility.
2. Why this exists: SEO reports tell what broke; Adticks measures what changed.
3. Measurement layers: search, AI, technical drag, content health, authority flow, competitors.
4. Deep dive engine: crawl, render, understand, prioritize.
5. Team views: technical SEO, content, agencies, SaaS/publishers.
6. Final CTA: start with a domain and expand into full visibility measurement.

## Design Guardrails

- Original copy only.
- No competitor visual cloning.
- No one-note blue palette; use blue for primary measurement, green for improvement, amber/red for risk.
- Avoid implementation-stack copy above the fold.
- Keep UI dense enough to feel operational, but not so dense that it becomes a crawler admin panel.
